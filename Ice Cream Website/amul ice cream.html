Amul Ice Cream

Amul Ice Cream was launched on 10th March, 1996 in Gujarat. The portfolio consisted of impulse products like sticks, cones, cups as well as take home packs and institutional/catering packs. Amul ice cream was launched on the platform of ‘Real Milk, Real Ice Cream’ given that it is a milk company and the wholesomeness of its products gives it a competitive advantage.

In 1997, Amul ice creams entered Mumbai, Chennai in 1998 and Kolkata and Delhi in 2002. Nationally it was rolled out across the country in 1999.

It has combated competition like Kwality Walls, Mother Dairy and achieved the No.1 position in the country. This position was achieved in 2001 and it has continued to remain at the top.

It has not only grown at a phenomenal rate but it has added a vast variety of flavours to its ever growing range. Currently it offers a selection of 220 products. Amul has always brought newness in its products and the same applies for ice creams.

In January 2007, Amul introduced SUGAR FREE & ProLife Probiotic Wellness Ice Cream, which was the first in India. This range of SUGAR FREE, LOW FAT Diabetic Delight & ProLife Probiotic Wellness Ice Cream is created for the health conscious.

Conclusion

In a short span of 6 years, Amul Ice Cream has become the No.1 ice cream brand in the country. It is now the only national brand and all other ice cream brands are regional. It has also achieved 38% share against 9% market share of HLL making it 4 times larger than its closest competitor.
